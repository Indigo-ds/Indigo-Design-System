    Information
    1. Use this service to find out the approved use of for a subject premises.
    2. You would need the following information to complete your registration:
      • Subject Premises details
      • Floor Plan
      • Your Particular details
    3. A search fee of $53.50 per enquiry applies. It takes around 7 working days for us to process your request, and a reply will be sent via email.
    4. If the subject premises is a private shophouse unit, you can immediately obtain information on approved use of the premises at URA SPACE (free-of-charge).
    5. This service does not advise if a Change of Use can be allowed (i.e. when a proposed use is different from the approved use). For details on the Change of Use application, please click here.
