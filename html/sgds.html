    Information

    You can find out the approved use of the subject premises you need. For private shophouse unit, you can immediately obtain information on approved use of the premises at URA SPACE (free-of-charge).

    You would need the following information to complete your application:

    1. Subject Premises details
    2. Floor Plan if available
    3. Your Particular details

    A search fee of $53.50 per enquiry applies. It takes around 7 working days for us to process your request, and a reply will be sent via email.

    If you want to check if a Change of Use can be allowed such as (when a proposed use is different from the approved use), please visit the Change of Use application page.
